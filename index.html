+ How long does Mehndi last on the skin?

Typically, Mehndi lasts on the skin for 1-3 weeks, depending on factors such as skin type, location of application, and aftercare. To prolong the longevity of your Mehndi, avoid frequent washing of the area and moisturize regularly with natural oils like coconut or olive oil.

+ Can Mehndi be removed quickly if needed?

While Mehndi naturally fades over time, there are methods to expedite its removal if necessary. Rubbing the area with a mixture of lemon juice and sugar or applying olive oil can help fade Mehndi more quickly. However, it's essential to be gentle to avoid skin irritation.
